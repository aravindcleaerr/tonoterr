The Story

Two Names, One Mission

Founded in December 2017, toN0tErr operates under two complementary brands:

toN0tErr

Tools to Nought Error — the umbrella brand encompassing all products, tools, and services aimed at driving errors toward zero. The “0” in the name represents the goal: zero errors.

CleaErr

Clear Errors — the consulting practice brand. Through CleaErr Tech Solutions, we deliver Technical Consulting, Design Consulting, and Embedded Engineering — working as an integral part of client engineering teams.

Every product, service, and consulting engagement we undertake shares a single purpose: to systematically reduce and eliminate errors. Errors can be human, machine, or system-originated. Our tools can be products, devices, processes, or applications. What unites them is the relentless drive toward zero.

Our Approach

Identify. Engineer. Eliminate.

A three-step methodology applied to every engagement, every tool, every solution.

1. Identify

Understand where errors originate. Is it a human process gap? A machine calibration drift? A system design flaw? Root cause analysis before solution design.

2. Engineer

Build tools, devices, processes, and applications that address root causes. Whether it's a hardware test jig, an AI-powered QC scanner, or an energy compliance platform — engineer the solution.

3. Eliminate

Drive error rates toward zero through deployment, monitoring, and continuous refinement. The goal isn't just detection — it's elimination.

Founding Heritage

Two Projects That Built the Zero-Error Philosophy

Before toN0tErr existed, two experiences shaped the principle that systematic errors require a systematic framework — not individual effort.

Precision Instrumentation

SCAD 508 — Indigenizing Precision Test Equipment

At Pyrodynamics, R&D and indigenization of the SCAD 508 strain measurement system eliminated India's dependence on imported precision test equipment. Pyrodynamics received the Technology Development Board Award from the President of India for this achievement. The SCAD 508 was the only Indian-developed instrument exhibited internationally in Germany.

Lesson: Precision is not a feature — it is an architecture decision. Every measurement system must be designed to eliminate the error, not just detect it.

Origin Idea

Lab Digitization — The Idea That Started toN0tErr

At Axiom Consulting, a pilot project to digitize three manual quality instruments at a P&G testing facility in Singapore grew into something unexpected. A reusable digitization framework — digital data capture, ADC modules for analog instruments, online analytics, and automated reporting — proved so effective that P&G commissioned the full lab (18 instruments), then ordered digitization of over 200 testing labs worldwide, making it a major business vertical for Axiom.

When toN0tErr was founded, this concept was the first product idea pursued — applied to medical laboratory diagnostics in India. The market proved harder than anticipated: fragmented instruments, entrenched referral economics, and cautious adoption. That year of pursuit shaped the deep understanding of why systematic errors are hard to eliminate and what it actually takes.

Lesson: Systematic errors don't require heroic effort to fix — they require a reusable framework. That insight is the founding purpose of toN0tErr.
