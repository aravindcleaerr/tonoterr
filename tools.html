Manufacturing QC

ProdSync

Production Scanner App

AI-powered manufacturing quality control and production tracking. Three role-based modes for different levels of quality assurance.

  • Barcode/QR scanning with quality status logging (Pass/Reject)
  • AI-powered defect analysis via Google Gemini Vision API
  • Photo documentation of defects with automated shift reports
  • Dual-process support: outward production + inward field returns
  • Offline capability with Google Sheets cloud sync
Gemini AI Barcode Scanner Google Sheets PWA
Manufacturing defects reaching customers → AI-caught at the line

Energy Management

VoltSpark

Industrial Energy Compliance Platform

All-in-one energy management and compliance platform for India's industrial sector — built for energy consultants, ESCOs, and manufacturing units.

  • 30+ compliance modules covering ZED, ISO 50001, and electrical safety
  • Utility bill analysis with auto-detection of penalties and anomalies
  • Pre-built ROI calculators and savings documentation
  • Compliance calendar with audit and certification deadline tracking
  • Multi-tenant SaaS with role-based access for consultants and clients

Part Inspection

PartOK

Mechanical Part Inspection Platform

AI-powered mechanical part inspection and quality management platform for manufacturing and engineering teams.

  • CAD file upload and parsing (STL, STEP, DXF) with 3D viewer
  • AI visual defect detection using YOLOv8 with photo evidence
  • Dimensional inspection with tolerance tracking per feature
  • PDF inspection report generation with pass/fail traceability
  • Real-time collaboration with multi-user WebSocket editing
YOLOv8 AI CAD Parser FastAPI React
Undetected mechanical defects reaching assembly → AI-inspected with full dimensional traceability

PCB Inspection

PCBok

AI-Powered PCB Inspector

Comprehensive PCB inspection and management platform with AI analysis, design rule checking, and component lifecycle tracking.

  • Gerber, KiCad, Altium, Eagle file parsing with interactive layer viewer
  • Design Rule Check (DRC) for trace width, clearance, and via rules
  • Component library with lifecycle tracking (active / NRND / EOL)
  • BOM cost estimation with supplier pricing integration
  • Board revision diff — side-by-side comparison of added/removed components
Gerber Parser DRC Engine FastAPI React + TypeScript
PCB design errors and component obsolescence escaping review → AI-checked before production

Functional Test Automation

ZeroTest

Production Test Management Platform

Zero-Defect | Zero-Config | Zero-to-Production — a complete production test management platform for hardware teams. Tracks DUTs, runs tests, and drives yield toward 100%.

  • DUT Registry — multi-industry device tracking across electronics, power, automotive, and industrial domains
  • Test Profiles — reusable templates with ordered steps, limits, and equipment requirements
  • Live Test Runner — real-time WebSocket streaming of measurements with pass/fail decisions
  • Equipment Manager — instrument tracking with calibration schedules
  • Standards Library — IPC, IEEE, IEC compliance linked to test profiles
FastAPI React + TypeScript WebSockets PostgreSQL
Production escapes from untested or under-tested devices → Every DUT tested, measured, and yield-verified
