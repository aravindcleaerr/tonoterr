Manufacturing QC

ProdSync

Production Scanner App

AI-powered manufacturing quality control and production tracking. Three role-based modes for different levels of quality assurance.

  • Barcode/QR scanning with quality status logging (Pass/Reject)
  • AI-powered defect analysis via Google Gemini Vision API
  • Photo documentation of defects with automated shift reports
  • Dual-process support: outward production + inward field returns
  • Offline capability with Google Sheets cloud sync
Gemini AI Barcode Scanner Google Sheets PWA
Manufacturing defects reaching customers → AI-caught at the line

Safety Training

MCQ Assessment

Industrial Safety Training Platform

Assessment platform delivering MCQ quizzes across critical safety domains with automated scoring and result tracking.

  • Electrical Safety (SFS 6002), First Aid, and EHS (PSK 6803:2024)
  • User authentication with name and employee ID
  • Auto-scoring with 70% pass threshold
  • Past results tracking and multiple quiz modules
  • Google Apps Script backend integration

Financial Tracking

FinSnap

Financial Transaction Logger

Mobile-friendly transaction logging for small businesses with photo evidence and intelligent data capture.

  • Receipts (income) and payments (expenses) with photo evidence
  • Smart paste: auto-detects monetary amounts from copied text
  • Payment mode selection (Cash, Cheque, NEFT)
  • Offline-first with Google Sheets sync
  • Transaction history with CSV and PDF export

  • Attendance history with filtering and reporting
  • Data sync to remote server
