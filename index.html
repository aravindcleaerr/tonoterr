What Sets Us Apart

Founding Heritage

SCAD 508 — Where the Precision-First Philosophy Was Forged

Before toN0tErr was founded, the zero-error standards that define our work today were built through hands-on R&D at Pyrodynamics — indigenizing the SCAD 508 strain measurement system. Pyrodynamics received the Technology Development Board Award from the President of India for this work. The SCAD 508 was the only Indian-developed instrument exhibited internationally in Germany.

AI-Powered

ProdSync: Manufacturing QC Scanner

AI-powered defect detection using Google Gemini Vision. Barcode scanning, quality status logging, and automated shift reports — driving manufacturing defects toward zero.

Viral

Idli ATM: Automated Food Robot

Hardware lead for India's viral fully-automated cooking and vending robot. 72 idlis per cycle, fully contactless. Covered by Business Today, The Quint, Scroll, Deccan Herald.

HealthTech

Radar-Based AI Health Monitoring

Currently leading hardware development for mmWave radar-based AI health monitoring in senior care — fall detection, vital signs, sleep analysis, and activity monitoring.
